## Common Beginner Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

### Mistake 1: Trying to build complex things too early

**Solution**: Master the basics first. Every expert was once a beginner.

### Mistake 2: Not practicing regularly

**Solution**: Code a little bit every day, even if it's just 15 minutes.

### Mistake 3: Getting discouraged by errors

**Solution**: Errors are your teachers! They tell you exactly what to fix.

### Mistake 4: Not reading error messages

**Solution**: Error messages are hints. Read them carefully.
